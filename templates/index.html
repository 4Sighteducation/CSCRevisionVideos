About the Series

Ten short videos that guide students through effective study and revision practices. Covering where, what, and how to revise effectively, this comprehensive series explores everything from designing the ideal study environment to selecting key topics and applying research-based revision strategies. Perfect for Key Stage 4-5 students looking to improve their exam preparation.
